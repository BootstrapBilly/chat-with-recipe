Simple Pasta Recipe

Servings: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Fresh basil leaves
- Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add crushed tomatoes and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Drain pasta and add to the sauce. Toss to combine.
6. Serve topped with fresh basil and parmesan cheese.
